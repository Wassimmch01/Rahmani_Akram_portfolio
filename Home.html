About

My name is Rahmani Akram, a filmmaker and visual storyteller. Through my lens, I capture moments of adventure, freedom, and raw emotion. Every place I explore, every face I meet, every silence I encounter becomes a scene worth telling. My work is more than just visuals — it’s about crafting real stories that make people feel, dream, and travel beyond the screen. I don’t just film what I see… I film what I feel.
